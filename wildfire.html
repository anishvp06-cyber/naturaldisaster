Wildfires

What to do:

    R.A.C.E. Fire Response Procedure

    You must follow the R.A.C.E. fire procedure before attempting to extinguish any fire.

  1. Rescue/Remove

    2. Rescue or remove any persons from the immediate scene

  2. Alert/Activate

    3. Pull the nearest alarm and call 911, then the following numbers:

    CRC: 617-353-2121

    BUMC: 617-414-6666

  3. Confine

    4. Close all doors to the hazard or fire area

  4. Extinguish/Evacuate

    5. Extinguish using the closest fire extinguisher if the fire impedes your evacuation.

    Evacuate to your designated meeting location.
